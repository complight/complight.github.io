    Seminars¶

    We organize virtual environments and computer graphics weekly seminar series. Virtual environments and computer graphics weekly seminar series is an exclusive event where we host experts across the industry and academia. Overall, seminars are a blend of internal and external presenters.

    Question

    If you are wondering how to get an invitation to the next seminar series, please do not hesitate to email Kaan Akşit or subscribe yourself to our mailing list (only within University College London's network).
